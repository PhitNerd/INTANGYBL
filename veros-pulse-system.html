Fatigue Vulnerability · Daily Signal
What is the Pulse Check-in?

Three questions, answered daily. Each one captures a different aspect of Fatigue Vulnerability — how cognitive depletion is affecting your decision-making right now.

Fatigue doesn't just make you tired. It narrows attention, lowers skepticism, and makes you more likely to comply with requests you'd normally question. The Pulse tracks that in real time so the system knows when your risk window is open.

Drift Engine · Live Analysis
What is the Drift Engine?

The Drift Engine watches for meaningful movement away from your behavioral baseline. A single elevated day is noise. A pattern across check-ins is signal. When fatigue scores shift consistently upward, your susceptibility rises — and the engine flags it before it becomes a problem.

It's the analytical layer between your daily check-in and your weekly briefing. It decides when current conditions are significant enough to warrant attention and action.
