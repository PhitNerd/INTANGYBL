The Position
NOT A
TRAINING
TOOL.

Security awareness training operates on a false premise: that repeated instruction moves people toward safer behavior. It doesn't — because behavior is not the root problem.

Behavior is the output of stable traits interacting with variable conditions. A person with high social compliance doesn't become less compliant because they completed a phishing module. Their compliance is a relational orientation — a stable architecture.

This distinction changes everything. A useful security instrument shouldn't try to train the trait. It should map the trait accurately, monitor the conditions continuously, and signal when their intersection becomes exploitable.

That's Veros. Not a training platform. A trait-relative risk instrument.

The Instrument
THE IBRI

The Intangybl Behavioral Risk Index. Four dimensions. Each one maps a stable trait architecture — not a behavior score, not a training outcome.

DIMENSION 01
FV
Fatigue Vulnerability
The rate and severity at which cognitive depletion degrades decision quality. High FV individuals show rapid instinct suppression under load — verification steps feel costly, urgency pressure feels legitimate.
Most situational of the four. Trait-level stress tolerance determines the floor — conditions determine the expression.
DIMENSION 02
SC
Social Compliance
A relational orientation — a stable tendency to weight authority, belonging, and others' expectations heavily in decision-making. Amplifies in hierarchical environments and under perceived urgency from trusted sources.
Cannot be trained away. Can be mapped precisely — so the conditions that activate it can be monitored and flagged.
DIMENSION 03
RR
Risk Rationalization
A cognitive style — the stable tendency to construct post-hoc justifications for decisions already made intuitively. Amplifies under high cognitive load. Makes over-explained, plausible-seeming requests more dangerous, not less.
Attackers who understand RR don't hide their rationale — they over-supply it.
DIMENSION 04
EA
Exposure Awareness
The closest to a knowledge and habit dimension — familiarity with attack patterns, domain verification instincts, and established security practice. The most trainable of the four.
Veros treats EA differently from the other three — because the mechanism of improvement is genuinely different here.
◈
The IBRI score is not a fixed label. It's a continuous reading of trait expression under current conditions. The architecture is what Veros maps. The conditions are what Veros watches.
